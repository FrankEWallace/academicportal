   - Breakdown of tuition, fees, paid amount
   - Clear "Fully Paid" or "Outstanding Balance" indicator

   - Policy details if verified
   - Expiry warning if approaching renewal

   - Filterable by semester
   - Download PDF receipts
   - Payment method shown
   - Clear status indicators
