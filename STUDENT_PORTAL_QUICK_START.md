##  Print Forms Page

### What You Can Do
 Print or download documents instantly (no waiting)

### Available Documents

#### Academic Documents
- **Admission Letter** - Your official university welcome letter
- **Academic Transcript** - Complete grade history (confidential)
- **Course Registration Form** - Current semester course list
- **Class Timetable** - Your weekly class schedule

#### Personal Documents
- **Student ID Card** - Official ID with photo
- **Accommodation Letter** - Hostel assignment (if applicable)

### How to Use
1. Click **Print** to open and print immediately
2. Click **Download** to save PDF to your computer
3. Check badge status to see if document is available

### Notes
- Payment receipts and invoices can be accessed from their respective pages
- All documents are in PDF format
- Some documents require active enrollment to generate

##  Document Requests Page

### What You Can Do
 Request official documents that need processing  
 Track request status  
 Download completed documents

### Document Types You Can Request

1. **Official Transcript** - Certified academic record
2. **Certificate of Enrollment** - Proof you're currently enrolled
3. **Certificate of Good Conduct** - Character reference
4. **Recommendation Letter** - From your academic advisor
5. **Completion Letter** - Proof you completed your program
6. **Clearance Certificate** - Shows no outstanding obligations
7. **Transfer Letter** - For transferring to another institution
8. **Other Document** - Any other official document

### How to Submit a Request

1. Click **"New Request"** button
2. Select **document type** from dropdown
3. Enter **purpose/reason** (required)
   - Example: "Application for graduate school"
   - Example: "Job application requirement"
4. Add **additional information** (optional)
   - Example: "Please address to ABC Corporation HR"
5. Click **"Submit Request"**

### Track Your Requests

Requests go through these stages:

-  **Pending** - Just submitted, awaiting review
-  **Processing** - Staff is preparing your document
-  **Approved** - Approved and being finalized
-  **Completed** - Ready! Click download button
-  **Rejected** - Request denied (reason shown)

### Processing Time
‚è±Ô∏è **3-5 business days** for most requests

### Download Completed Documents
1. Find your request in the list
2. Look for **"Completed"** status
3. Click **"Download"** button
4. Document saves to your computer

##  Tips & Tricks

### Print Forms
-  Download first, then print from your PDF reader for better quality
-  Check if you have Adobe PDF Reader or similar installed
-  Some documents show "unavailable" if requirements aren't met
-  Financial documents link to payment history for specific receipts

### Document Requests
-  Be specific in your reason - helps with processing
-  Include recipient information in additional info if needed
-  Check status regularly - no email notifications yet
-  Download immediately when completed - links may expire
-  Processing fees may apply for some documents

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Print Button Not Working?
1. Check if pop-up blockers are disabled
2. Try download instead, then print from PDF reader
3. Make sure you're logged in

### Download Not Starting?
1. Check browser download settings
2. Allow downloads from this site
3. Try a different browser

### Document Shows "Unavailable"?
- **Course Registration**: You need active enrollment
- **Timetable**: You need active enrollment
- **Accommodation Letter**: Only if you have hostel assignment
- **Payment Receipts**: Access from payment history page

### Request Stuck on "Pending"?
- Normal processing time is 3-5 business days
- If urgent, contact registrar's office
- Check during business hours (Mon-Fri)

### Can't Download Completed Request?
1. Click "View Details" first
2. Look for download button in details
3. Contact registrar if download link is missing
